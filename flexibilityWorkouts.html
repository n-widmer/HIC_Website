Flexibility and Balance Workouts

Flexibility Exercises

Forward Fold Stand with feet hip-width apart, fold forward from the hips, reaching toward the ground or your shins.
Seated Forward Bend Sit on the floor with legs extended, hinge at the hips to fold forward, reaching toward your toes.

Balance Exercises

Single-Leg Balance Stand on one leg, keeping the other leg lifted off the ground, and hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Switch sides.
Tree Pose Stand on one leg, place the sole of the other foot against the inner thigh or calf, and bring hands together at chest level. Hold for balance.
Bird Dog Start on hands and knees, extend one arm forward and the opposite leg back, keeping the back straight. Hold for a few seconds, then switch sides.
