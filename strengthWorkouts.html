Strength Workouts

Chest Workouts

Bench Press The bench press is a compound exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Lie flat on a bench and lift a barbell up and down.
Push-Ups Push-ups are a bodyweight exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Place your hands on the ground and push your body up and down.

Back Workouts

Deadlifts Deadlifts are a compound exercise that targets the lower back, glutes, hamstrings, and core. Stand with a barbell and lift it up from the ground to hip level.
Pull-Ups Pull-ups are a bodyweight exercise that targets the back, shoulders, and arms. Hang from a bar and pull your body up until your chin reaches the bar.

Arm Workouts

Bicep Curls Bicep curls target the biceps and forearms. Hold dumbbells in your hands and curl them up towards your shoulders.
Tricep Dips Tricep dips target the triceps and shoulders. Sit on a bench and lower your body by bending your elbows.

Leg Workouts

Squats Squats target the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and lower your body down as if sitting in a chair.
Lunges Lunges target the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Step forward with one leg and lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.

Abs Workouts

Crunches Crunches target the rectus abdominis. Lie on your back with your knees bent and lift your upper body towards your knees.
Planks Planks target the core muscles, including the abs and lower back. Hold a push-up position with your body straight and supported by your elbows and toes.
